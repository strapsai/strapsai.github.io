The Team

Team Chiron consists of professors, physicians, researchers, scientists, engineers and students with a wide range of experience in robotics and medicine

We Are Recruiting

Team Chiron is always looking for new members who are passionate about creating robots for real-world application and impact. We are always open to collaborations and partnerships, especially young telents.
