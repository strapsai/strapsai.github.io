Our Expertise

Autonomous Robotics

Our robotics experts develop advanced navigation and perception systems for complex, unstructured environments.

Artificial Intelligence

Our AI researchers develop machine learning models for casualty detection, medical assessment, and triage decision support.

Emergency Medicine

Our medical professionals provide clinical expertise and validate our systems against established triage protocols and standards.

Systems Integration

Our systems engineers combine diverse technologies into cohesive, reliable platforms that function effectively in challenging conditions.
