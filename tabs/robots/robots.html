Our Robot Fleet

Coordinated aerial and ground robots work together using AI to detect casualties, assess injuries, and support critical decisions in dangerous environments. We're continuously improving and integrating our system for better performance.

Mission Control

CHIRON Command System

Our central command and control platform coordinates all robots in the field and processes the information they collect. This system serves as the center for the entire operation, providing first responders with actionable intelligence for optimal resource allocation.

Data Fusion AI Triage Tactical Mapping Resource Optimization Situational Awareness

CHIRON Quadruped Responder

Our ground-based quadruped robot is designed to navigate challenging terrain and reach casualties for detailed assessment. On top of latest legged robotics technology from Spot, we've equipped it with specialized sensors and a manipulation arm for close-range casualty assessment.
